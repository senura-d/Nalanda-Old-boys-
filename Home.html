About Us

Who We Are

The Old Nalandians Association of South Australia (ONASA) is more than just an alumni group; it is a lifelong brotherhood rooted in the values of our prestigious alma mater.

Our mission is to sustain and strengthen the bonds between former students residing in South Australia, providing a platform for professional networking, social fellowship, and charitable initiatives that support both our school back home and our local community here in Adelaide.
