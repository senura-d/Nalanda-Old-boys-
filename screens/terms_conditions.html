2. Membership & Conduct

Membership is open to all former students of Nalanda College living in South Australia. To maintain a professional and supportive community environment, members must adhere to the following rules:

  • Account Integrity: You must provide accurate and complete information during registration. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.
  • Brotherhood Conduct: Members must interact with others respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language in association forums or events will lead to immediate termination of membership.
  • Prohibited Use: You may not use association resources for unauthorized commercial purposes or political campaigning without explicit written consent from the executive committee.
  • Account Termination: ONASA reserves the right to suspend or terminate membership at its sole discretion for violations of this code of conduct.

3. Association Events & Fees

ONASA organizes various social, charitable, and networking events throughout the year. The following terms apply to registrations and dues:

  • Membership Dues: Annual membership fees are non-refundable and are utilized for the operational costs of the association and charitable initiatives.
  • Event Registration: Payment for ticketed events must be made in full at the time of booking. Registrations are typically non-transferable unless specified otherwise.
  • Refund Policy: Refund requests for events must be submitted at least 7 days prior to the event date. Processing fees may apply.
  • Event Cancellation: ONASA reserves the right to cancel or reschedule events due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, full refunds or credits will be provided.
