Our Identity

Our Legacy

The journey of the Old Nalandians Association of South Australia began with a shared vision of brotherhood across oceans. On March 23, 1991, a group of dedicated alumni gathered for an inaugural meeting that would lay the foundation for a community far from home.

Founded by visionary alumni who sought to preserve the "Nalandian Spirit" in the heart of South Australia, the association has grown from a handful of members to a vibrant network of professionals, mentors, and friends. Our founding members established a core mission: to support each other and give back to our alma mater.

flag

Our Mission

To foster a lifelong connection among Old Nalandians in South Australia, providing a platform for professional growth, cultural celebration, and philanthropic support for Nalanda College.

visibility

Our Vision

To be the most influential and supportive alumni community in the region, recognized for our commitment to excellence, brotherhood, and the empowerment of future generations.

What it means to be a Nalandian

Nalanda College Colombo is more than just a school; it is an institution that forged our character and defined our values. Being a Nalandian means carrying the weight of a rich heritage—a tradition of academic brilliance, sporting prowess, and unyielding patriotism.

"One School, One Brotherhood, One Community." This ethos drives everything we do at ONASA, ensuring that the fire ignited at Nalanda continues to burn bright in South Australia.
